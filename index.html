Products

CodeHSM

CodeHSM is a high security application and code signing service that handles your digital signature needs for Android, iOS, MacOS, Windows and more. We provide a simple API coupled with a highly secure key management service to handle cryptographic signing for you, so you don't have to worry about the security of your most important keys.

Sault

Sault is a safety deposit box for your most valuable digital secrets. We handle the operations, storage and long-term archiving of your data, and you control its security by keeping the keys.

On-Demand Engineering

We provide engineering services to help you design, build and run security applications that answer your most complex use-cases. Contact us for more information.

Security Embedding, Architecture & Training

Security Embedding

We work closely with your engineering teams to handle operational security work and train your organization until you've reached the point of hiring your own security team (we'll help you do that, too). We review new projects, fix issues in your infrastructure, provide risk reporting to upper management, etc. It's a great fit for startups and companies that have not yet reached the size to have in-house security.

Security Architecture

Decades of experience building, running and security complex infrastructure are at your disposal to design and review the security of your next project. Contact us when you're exploring your next big idea, or need help reviewing future or existing infrastructure.

Training

We provide customized security training to help your organizations increase its security skills. We cover the entire scope of building and operating complex web platforms, from web applications security, to infrastructure hardening, to security testing, to risk management. Contact us for more information.
